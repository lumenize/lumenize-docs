Get started with Lumenize in minutes. Choose the plan that fits your needs and start building your backend today.

## Create Your Account

1. **Visit the signup page** at [app.lumenize.dev/signup](https://app.lumenize.dev/signup)
2. **Choose your plan**:
   - **Developer** - Free tier for learning and prototyping
   - **Starter** - Perfect for small applications and MVPs
   - **Professional** - For growing businesses and teams
   - **Enterprise** - Custom solutions for large organizations

3. **Provide your information**:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Organization name (optional for Developer tier)

4. **Verify your email** by clicking the link we send you

## Authentication Options

Choose how you'd like to sign in:

- **Email/Password** - Traditional authentication
- **GitHub** - Sign in with your GitHub account
- **Google** - Sign in with your Google account
- **SSO** - Enterprise single sign-on (Enterprise tier only)

## What Happens Next?

After signup, you'll be taken to the Lumenize dashboard where you can:

1. **Create your first project** - Define your application's backend
2. **Set up your organization** - Invite team members and configure access
3. **Define your schemas** - Start modeling your data
4. **Get API credentials** - Connect your frontend application

## Free Tier Limits

The Developer tier includes:

- **1 project** with unlimited schemas
- **10,000 API requests** per month
- **1 GB storage** 
- **Community support**
- **All core features** (schemas, real-time, MCP)
